About RecurKit

RecurKit is a dead simple recurring task manager built for one type of person: the solo operator.

If you're a freelancer, a one-person business, a landlord, a side-hustler, or anyone who has stuff that needs to get done on a regular schedule — this is for you.

Why we built this

Every task manager out there wants you to manage projects, invite teammates, set up boards, create labels, and configure workflows. That's great if you're a team of 50. But if you're just one person who needs to remember to:

  • Send invoices on the 1st of every month
  • Check inventory every Thursday
  • Renew your domain name on March 15th
  • Pay quarterly taxes
  • Back up your files every Sunday

…then you don't need Notion. You don't need Asana. You don't need Monday. You need RecurKit.

How it works

  1. Add a task — give it a name and tell it how often it repeats (daily, weekly, monthly, yearly, or just once).
  2. Check it off — when you've done it, click the circle. RecurKit automatically calculates when it's next due.
  3. Stay on top of it — open RecurKit each day to see what's due. Overdue tasks are highlighted so nothing slips through.

Your data stays with you

RecurKit stores everything in your browser's local storage. No accounts. No cloud sync. No sign-ups. Your tasks live on your device. Period.

Features

  • ✅ Daily, weekly, monthly, yearly, and one-time tasks
  • ✅ Auto-calculates next due date when you complete a task
  • ✅ Overdue task highlighting
  • ✅ Filter by today, upcoming, overdue, or all
  • ✅ Quick stats dashboard
  • ✅ Works on phone, tablet, and desktop
  • ✅ No sign-up required
  • ✅ 100% free
