About Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

The short version

RecurKit does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your tasks are saved in your browser's local storage and never leave your device.

Data we collect

None. RecurKit is a client-side application. We do not have servers, databases, or user accounts. All data you enter stays in your browser.

Local storage

RecurKit uses your browser's localStorage feature to save your tasks. This data:

  • Is stored only on your device
  • Is never transmitted to any server
  • Can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser data
  • Is not accessible to other websites

Cookies

RecurKit does not use cookies. Period.

Analytics

RecurKit does not use any analytics or tracking services. We have no way to know who you are or what tasks you create.

Children's privacy

RecurKit is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Datenschutzrichtlinie

RecurKit sammelt, speichert oder überträgt keine personenbezogenen Daten. Ihre Aufgaben werden im lokalen Speicher Ihres Browsers gespeichert und verlassen nie Ihr Gerät.

Keine. RecurKit ist eine clientseitige Anwendung. Wir haben keine Server, Datenbanken oder Benutzerkonten. Alle Daten, die Sie eingeben, bleiben in Ihrem Browser.

La version courte

RecurKit ne s'adresse pas aux enfants de moins de 13 ans. Nous ne collectons sciemment aucune information auprès des enfants.
